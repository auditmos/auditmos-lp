powiadomienia.info is a personal notification hub for households: enter an address for waste-collection schedules, add the family's important dates, connect a Telegram bot — privately or in a family group chat — and receive a short message exactly when it matters.

The product is in early access, with an email waitlist on the public site.

Small consumer products like this one are where delivery habits get stress-tested end to end: onboarding, scheduling correctness, message timing, and the unglamorous work of keeping a service quietly reliable.
