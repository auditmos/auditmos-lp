wizytowka.link automatically generates public informational pages for local Polish businesses that have no website: name, category, address, opening hours, a map, and a description — covering 95,000+ Polish localities with 24 visual themes.

Instead of exposing a phone number, each page carries an "ask the assistant" button. The assistant answers from the page's own data, states clearly that it does not represent the business, and records transcripts and intent summaries as demand signals in a private operator panel.

Running a product end to end — generation pipeline, SEO surface, assistant behavior, and the honesty constraints around impersonation — is the same discipline Auditmos applies to client delivery.
